For many business owners... the words "business plan" conjure up thoughts of a painful, overwhelming task they'd rather avoid at all costs! (And many do!)

But any business without a well-thought-out plan is like a ship without a rudder - you'll get SOMEWHERE, certainly, but not likely where you WANT to.

Now there's no longer any reason to fear this important task because Linda McLean is "going public" with her Snapshot Business Planning system - until now reserved exclusively for her private clients.

With 27 years as a business coach, and a passion for systems, organization, sound business practices, and problem-solving, Linda has come up with a fool-proof method for creating a business plan that’s doable for anyone, in any industry, in any size business.

And now, in this book, she leads you clearly through the entire process – from envisioning your ideal business to recognizing your accomplishments along the way, and celebrating each one, big or small. And every step in between! (Twelve simple steps altogether – one for each facet of your business. And when you’re done, it will fit within just two or three pages!)

With helpful worksheets throughout, and a summary of “next steps” you can take, Snapshot Business Planning is all you need to craft a practical, workable plan for YOUR business, and put it into action immediately!
