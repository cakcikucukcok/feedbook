Since Molandas divorce, she has been a virtuous woman in order to retain the respect of her teenage son, Rockean. Secretly, she has been praying to God for her Boaz. Unbeknown to Molanda, the good Reverend Isaac Joshiea has come to town on a mission. As he takes the pulpit and places his Bible on the podium he scans the congregation and his eyes rest upon Molanda he is moved. He asks God for confirmation as their eyes lock. Her jealous sister, Neola, watches Molanda shift her full hips in her seat as she begins to fan herself. Noticing Molandas reaction, Neola believes that the Reverend could possibly take her sister away from her. Neola survives off of the love, affection, and attention that Molanda dotes upon her at all costs. No, she wasnt quite sure she was ready to share Molanda regardless of how long her sister had been without a man. Neola knew she would have to devise a plan to get the Reverends attention off of Molanda and on to her and whatever way Neola needed for that to happen she didnt have a problem with.
