Abomination, defined by the dictionary as "a disgusting or loathsome thing, a feeling of disgust; loathing."

A more perfect description could not be located to describe Clyde Macon. A man of sheer brutality, sadistic sadism, and his insatiable hatred for all women. A twisted mind combined together with drugs and the uncontrollable urge to kill constitutes the beginning brush strokes of a painting from hell. A psychopathic killer on the loose preying on innocent women with his vampire's insatiable lust for their blood. A man with no soul, no compassion or feelings pertaining to the destruction of human life.

Clyde Macon survived his childhood abuse, inflected not only by his father but uncle Charles as well. The sexual abuse endured time after time from his uncle's all-night sleepovers. The death of his beloved mother before his eyes at the hands of his father, being forced to watch her bleed to death after having her throat slit...

But they would pay, every slut in the world, which to him were nothing but human garbage. They would all pay; he would damn sure see to that. Nothing was so important as ridding the world of the female filth and garbage that now cluttered the face of God's green earth.
