As the clock strikes thirteen on human civilization Evan A’con finds himself curiously displaced in time confronting what remains of humanity.  Cid the soldier finds herself on an impossible mission, possessing an intrigue which runs deeper than the chasms depths.  Jace and his merry band of traders stumble upon a nightmare from which no one has ever escaped.  Cataclysmic events have been set in motion and it is up to Evan, Cid, and Jace to decide what comes next.

Follow their paths in this post-apocalyptic tale centered on Terra Nova, a planet humans long ago colonized.  Read as the events unfold and the mystery of the Chasms light comes to bare in this part structured novel.  The story jumps between characters each unraveling the mystery, trying to stay alive on a barren world, eventually their paths will cross but the story doesn’t end there.
