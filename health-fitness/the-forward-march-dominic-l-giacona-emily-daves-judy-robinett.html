Where are you? Are you lost? Wandering? Wondering what’s next? Do you ache for something more out of life? Do you just need a direction? A plan...a purpose?

In 2012, Dominic Giacona graduated from high school with zero plans for his future. Instead of staying stuck, Dom decided to embrace drastic change and enlisted in the US Navy. In five short years, he went from wandering teen to purpose-driven sailor to product inventor.

The Forward March is the culmination of Dom’s experience of taking action against the unknown. It is both a mindset and a methodology, one that’s relatable, applicable, and motivating.

If you ache for more than where you are right now, don’t stay stuck. The Forward March is your path to passion and purpose. Stop waiting and start pursuing. Your time is now.
