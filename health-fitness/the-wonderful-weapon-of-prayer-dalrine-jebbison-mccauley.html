The Wonderful Weapon of Prayer is written out of a plethora of experiences that the author has been through over a period of years. These situations have allowed her to know and experience God in and from different perspectives. The book is done to help its readers to learn and understand the effectiveness of prayer. The author captures the usefulness of prayers, and how prayers that are coupled with faith and worship bring one into total favour with God. This favour helps us to find the will of God for our lives. The Wonderful Weapon of Prayer reveals that in praying we must be consistent, persistent, fervent and earnest in our approach. This book reveals to us some of the promises of God after finding Gods will. The book helps us to know how to pray so that we can do the work of God. It also reveals the secret of knowing how to go about doing Gods will through prayer. Finally, The Wonderful Weapon of Prayer illuminates the soul to realize that whatever is born of God overcomes the world since being born of God helps us to be people of a healthy personhood. Besides, we are reassured by His promises daily.
