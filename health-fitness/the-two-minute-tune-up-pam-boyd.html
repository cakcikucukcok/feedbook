Instead of getting out of bed and hoping today will be a good day, you can choose the day you want to have. You can control your own emotional response to the people and the things in your life. In The Two-Minute Tune-Up, author Pam Boyd provides a series of inspirational quotes, messages, and personal anecdotes to help you set a positive tone for every day for an entire year.

Boyd, an international consultant and speaker, shows how, in less than two minutes each day, you can overcome negative thoughts and feelings by reading the daily selection and taking a minute to reflect and apply its message to your circumstances. Its about reclaiming personal power and regaining emotional dominance.

The Two-Minute Tune-Up serves as your daily port of renewal: a place to recharge, flip your switch from idle to full power, and realign the wheels of your life.
