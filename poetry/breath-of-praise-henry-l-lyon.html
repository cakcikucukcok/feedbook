In the beginning, God gave us the breath of life. Now we, with every breath, need to praise God and all His creation.

His breath, we breathe, should be a reminder of His love and mercy that He pours out on all mankind.

How blessed we are to be sharing the breath of our Holy, perfect GOD.

Because these poems envelop many situations, they all can be applied in our everyday life and give us solutions to our problems with answers found in the Bible. God is here to give you, not only His breath, but Salvation through His Son, JESUS CHRIST. Please join me in praising our Lord for His free, grace-filled, breath of life. With each breath we take, PRAISE YE the LORD!
