Can love be lawful? Do futile experiences produce afflictions of love? Does deprivation kindle the fire of love? Can love persist?

This collection explores harmony and accordance in love, romantic love, loving with no disgrace, harmony in marriage, the secret of married life. It considers vacation and recovering emotion for the sake of a happy marriage and the obscure natures of men and women.

The warmth of the beloved can be found among the verses. The poet also addressed his feelings in a musical form, speaking about the joy of meeting his beloved and describing the types of love and its different stages.
