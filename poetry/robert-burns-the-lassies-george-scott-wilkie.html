Some were written as love poems or songs, intended to sway the heart of whoever had caught his eye, others in honour of a more casual acquaintance whose beauty or talents had impressed him in some way. Others were composed simply as a form of thank you letter for gifts or hospitality that he may have received. Robert Burns "?? The Lassies is a collection of all these poems, each accompanied by a detailed history of Burns' relationship with the subject.
