SOUL PRINTS

Soul prints is a collection of my private reflections, Of my souls personal journey here on Mother Earth, Of my many experiences and my personal connections, Of my multilayered being, my beliefs, and all their worth.

This beautiful collection takes form in many ways: From sweet love and laughter it will change to sad tears, But all will come from honesty, reflecting different days Of living humanly, not wanting judgment from my peers.

My intentions come from sharing in an imperfect place, Hoping that the various pieces, with so much diversity, Will reach out in this dimension, without a need to race, Simmer, digest, and finally resonate with many a new face.

All I ask of you is to hold, in all your head and heart, One hundred percent compassion, and you will play the perfect part!
