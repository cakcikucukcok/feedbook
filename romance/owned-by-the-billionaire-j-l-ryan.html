Frantic after getting fired, BBW Marisa Jamison is relieved when she's offered the perfect job at a prestigious law firm. There, she meets charismatic billionaire, Jeffrey Bradley.

There's only one problem...he's so sleazy that she wonders if the job is even worth it. Even so, she fantasizes about having mind-blowing sex with him and gets all sweaty at the very thought of him.

Curvy girl Marisa knows that Jeffrey isn't interested in her "type," and would never give her a second look. She's always been self-conscious about her weight, and has yet to find a guy who loves her for her sensual curves.

There's something about Marisa that Jeffrey can't resist, and he becomes obsessed with her. Big girls have never been his thing, but ever since they met, he craves her.

Is his desire for Marisa simply the result of a workplace crush, or is he hiding a scandalous heart-pounding secret that could jeopardize their well-being?
