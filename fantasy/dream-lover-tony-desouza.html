In this story our main character, a middle aged executive, charming husband and loving father and grandfather, boasts about his ability to continue with interrupted dreams. As fate would have it he meets a pretty young thing who happens to be his physical therapist. They have an affair and fall in love. Of course, at times it may be only in his dreams. For dreams and reality get confusing and the reader is hard pressed to differentiate one from the other.Time goes by and his lover pressures him to break off with his wife but he procrastinates; after all he has the best of both worlds. So he continues to play with fire in order to lead his two lives. Then there's the secretary at work with whom he has developed a close working relationship. His lover does not trust him and soon she develops a rage of jealousy that reaches a breaking point. How will this love affair end? Will he get burnt or is he only dreaming? You will have to read on and decide for yourself.So Happy Dreaming!
