Life takes an unexpected turn and you feel lost.

Why did God allow the bully to crush your joy?

How can you move through pain and suffering to delight in God?

Child of God, Delight in Him offers signposts

on your journey to the Fathers heart.

Follow along through art journaling, coloring pages,

the creative concept of mind-mapping Gods names,

and Carries story of her butterfly, Joy. Associating

and connecting God's names in this unique way will

open a kaleidoscope of emotions that will deepen

your experiences of prayer, worship and Bible study.

Treasure the serendipities along the way,

knowing that He will soon crush

the bully under your feet.
