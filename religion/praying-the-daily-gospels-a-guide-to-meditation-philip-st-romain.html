Discover a new love for Scripture! Many people have found a new devotion for the Word of God in Scripture and are eager to understand and prayerfully reflect on what Scripture calls them to become. However, learning to pray with Scripture does not always come easily. Focusing on the gospel passage for the Church's weekday liturgy, St. Romain gives the Scripture source for that day's liturgy, followed by brief commentary and reflection, to use as springboards for personal meditation. Those who wish to grow in their prayer life and become more attuned to the seasons of the liturgical year will find a wonderful guide in this spiritual companion.
