Primary school children are required to learn about Christianity and local churches are often keen to help, but don't know where to start. RE Active Church provides a four-year cycle of workshops that local churches can offer to children at Key Stage 2 (school years 3 to 6, ages 7 to 11). The tried-and-tested material covers Christmas, Easter and stories about Jesus, and includes everything you need to run sessions in your local church. The book also contains all the templates needed for the craft activities and additional material for use with nursery and Key Stage 1 children.
