I try to live my life as honest as possible but yet I know I'm not perfect. This book isn't meant to judge anyone or put anyone down, and I also try to live according to my words. I don't want to live under, "Do as I say and not as I do", I follow my own words just as much as I encourage the next person. This book was meant to show that anybody can do anything they want all you have to do is take charge and stop waiting for things to happen. I love talking about real things that everyone can relate to and stimulating the mind, so I hope this accomplishes that goal and encourages a positive change in the way people think. Love Always!
