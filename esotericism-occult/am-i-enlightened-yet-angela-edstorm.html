Angela Edstrom shares her healing journey from fear to love in this lighthearted account of her lifelong struggle to find peace. After being diagnosed with lupus in 1989, she began a quest for healing and spiritual enlightenment. Years of searching for a simple way to find the healing she so desperately wanted prompted her to create a plan that would forever change her life. Using spiritual ideas, she devised the Necessary Accessory plan, which focuses on one spiritual practice at a time over the course of a week. This plan is devised to be flexible for anyone to use and can be changed to suit individual healing journeys. After following the plan for a week you will begin to uncover a greater sense of spirit, and you will feel your life changing for the better. Angelas story will show that anyone can find enlightenmentand that everyone is entitled to live a joyful, peaceful life.
