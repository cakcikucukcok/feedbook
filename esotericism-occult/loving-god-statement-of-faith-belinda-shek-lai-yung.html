LOVING GOD: Statement of faith is the first book of the LOVING GOD series. It is to introduce the one and only one true everlasting God to peoples. And the LOVING GOD series is about the true everlasting God, how to obey His commandments, how to implement His commandments in the midst of their lives, and how to acknowledge His grace and mercy and the eternal life is the reward. The series is to help ones life living in peace and having hope in God through the Lord Christ even though ones life may seem hopelessness. The LOVING GOD series is indeed not just for Christians but also for non-Christians to have hope in times of troubles or difficulties or disappointments.
