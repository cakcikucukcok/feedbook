They say dentists have the highest suicide rate amongst professionals, and Dr Bruce Walter McCrae, B.D.Sc., well … he’s a troubled dentist. The monotony of his job (teeth, teeth, teeth … all day, every day) and the resentful, estranged relationship he has with his wife, builds to a point where he becomes bitter. Really bitter.

When he accuses his wife of killing their only son, he takes out his anguish not just on her … but his patients too. As he becomes plagued with haunting visions, he is taken to a

different, darker place – a place where he must come to grips with his own past … and his own future. After forty years, those events back then still haunt him, and as the

story comes to a close, Dr Bruce McCrae commits his last horrifying act.

Part horror, part suspense and part mystery. It will also have you guessing to the end. Will you be able to find ‘her’ before it’s too late?

Don’t be afraid. Open wide and let’s see what you will find …
