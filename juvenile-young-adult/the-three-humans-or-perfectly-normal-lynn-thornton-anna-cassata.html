The Goldilocks mythos has long been part of our culture, but a look through the eyes of Baby (or in this case, Youngest) Bear has been sadly missing. In this fun retelling, a bored Youngest Bear tires of helping his father and wanders off. At the same time, the Human family is going through their regular morning routine . . . until they end up going for a walk to let breakfast cool, leaving the stage clear for adventure.

Follow Youngest Bear through a day that starts normally enough for both the Bear family and the Human family but quickly becomes, as the reader might guess, not so normal after all.
